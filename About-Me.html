Hello! If you've stumbled across this website my name is Bradley Jester, I'm a current student of the UW Professional & Continuing Education Coding Boot Camp and an alumni of Seattle University. At the end of 2020, in the midst of the Covid-19 pandemic, I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication. Originally unsure of what direction I wanted to take my career, I began working for Nordstrom as a customer service representative. I'm super grateful for my time spent with Nordstrom so far: I've met so many amazing people and continued to improve upon the communication soft skills I gained at Seattle University. However, earlier this year I wanted to make a pivot to an industry that allows me to continually learn and utilize my creativity and problem solving skills more. After much research I decided I wanted to learn full stack web development and chose UW's Coding Boot Camp. I'm super excited to continue learning about browser based technologies and for later in the course when we dive into API design!
